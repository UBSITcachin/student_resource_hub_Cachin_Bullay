About Student Resource Hub

The Student Resource Hub is a platform designed to help students find, share, and manage educational resources across various subjects, including programming, design, and math.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide a centralized hub where students can easily discover high-quality learning materials, contribute their own resources, and connect with peers.

Features

  • Browse a collection of curated educational resources
  • Submit your own learning materials
  • Filter resources by category
  • Detailed descriptions and links for easy access
